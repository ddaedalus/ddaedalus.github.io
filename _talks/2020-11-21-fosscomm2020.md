E.Axiotis and I discussed our vision about an AI-enabled method for supporting the ethnopharmacological research through the development of the open source system. We proposed the development of an open source community and system that would help researchers find ethnopharmacological references on PubMed. Because of big data challenges, a focused search for collecting a large amount of specific publications on the Web is a quite difficult task for a researcher. However, with the powers of Machine Learning, an intelligent agent, under the name of Focused Crawler, can search efficiently for such documents utilizing only a few input seed documents, that the researcher is interested in a priori, as its starting point.
